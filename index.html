INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY & MANAGEMENT

Institute of Technology & Management (SSBES’ ITM), Nanded, was established in the year 1997. The Institute received an approval of Government of Maharashtra, All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi & is affiliated to Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathawada University, Nanded. The Institute offers two postgraduate programmes viz. MCA and MBA and three Graduate programmes viz. BCA, BBA, B.Sc. (Hospitality Studies). The institute is situated in the heart of Nanded City.

Institute of Technology and Management is committed to prepare skilled software and management professionals with high promise to act with determination, judgment and integrity in the responsible positions of leadership in the complex global business environment and who are dedicated to offer their services to various companies, society, humanity and nation.

The establishment of such infrastructure was made possible due to generosity of Late Hon. Dr. Shankarraoji Chavan (Ex. Home Minister of India), founder President of Shri Sharda Bhavan Education Society, Nanded. The Institute is making its progress under the noble guidance of Hon. Ashok Shankarraoji Chavan (Ex-Chief Minister, Maharashtra State), President of Shri Sharda Bhavan Education Society, Nanded, Hon. Mrs. Amita Ashokraoji Chavan, Vice President of Shri Sharda Bhavan Education Society, Nanded and Hon. D.P. Savant, Secretary of Shri Sharda Bhavan Education Society, Nanded.

Shri Sharda Bhavan Education Society’s Institute of Technology & Management, Nanded, is running successfully & maintaining excellent academic track record since its inception. This institute is giving almost 100% results and sharing sizable merit list of all courses in the Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded.
